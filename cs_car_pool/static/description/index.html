An app to manange shared vehicles across your organisation. Unlike the Fleet app which is concerned with assiging vehciles to specific drivers and managing the serivcing etc. this app enables the tracking of shared vechiles, recording to trips made, and booking vechiles ahead fo time.

Assume your business receives a traffic infridgement notice, you will quickly be able to see who was driving that vehicle at that time!
