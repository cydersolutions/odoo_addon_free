Cyder Travel Log

The Cyder Travel Log add important fields to keep track of each trip the vehicle makes.

Not all vehicles are used exclusively for business purposes and. depending on the arrangement with the employer, the driver may the option of personal use.

To accommodate these situation, we have added the following fields to the odometer reading:

  • Business
  • Tolls
  • Notes (optional)
  • Start odometer reading

Each trip can now be logged and identified as to the nature of the trip.
